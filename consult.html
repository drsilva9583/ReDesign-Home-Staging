Frequently Asked Questions

Most projects are completed within 1-3 days depending on the size of the home and scope of work. A full staging typically takes one full day, while a redesign consultation may only require a few hours.

Every package includes an initial walkthrough, a customized staging plan, furniture and décor placement, and a final styling review. Higher-tier packages add furniture rental, professional photography coordination, and extended rental periods.

Pricing depends on the size of the property and the services selected. We offer three packages — Standard, Professional, and Expert — with full details on our Services page. Book a free consultation for a personalized quote.

For occupied homes, we work around your schedule and only need access to the areas being staged. For vacant properties, our team can work independently with a key or lockbox access.

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks before your desired staging date. For listings going live during peak season (spring/summer), 3-4 weeks notice is ideal to ensure availability.
